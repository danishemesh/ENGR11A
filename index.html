Post reflection

Coming to ENGR 11A, I was very excited to learn how to 3D print and design different objects. In the nametag project, I had much fun creating my first-ever 3D print and succeeding on my very first try. On the other hand, the second print was a lot more complicated. I did many tries but still haven't succeeded in finding a print that will fit the requirements perfectly. While searching intensively for a new print, I at last found a perfect sort of action figure-looking print that ended up being my final print. One of my future goals is to become an entrepreneur, make different products that will solve problems, and make the world a better place. While being in this class, my expectations have excited as we are learning not just about designing but also about creating a website. This leaves me with a feeling that I am getting closer and closer to making my dream a reality.
